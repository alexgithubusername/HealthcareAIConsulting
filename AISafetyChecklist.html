APP Medical Students

Insights: APP Medical Students

Early exposure to AI tools is essential. Students should learn to use interactive simulations, verify clinical outputs against trusted sources, and develop the habit of critically evaluating AI recommendations to mitigate bias from the start.

Residents

Insights: Residents

Residents must adeptly merge AI-driven insights with hands-on clinical experience. Critical evaluation of AI outputs and active monitoring for biases are key to safe integration into patient care.

Fellows

Insights: Fellows

Fellows are expected to lead by example in both advanced clinical decision-making and research. They should critically evaluate AI systems, contribute to policy development, and mentor others in aligning technology with ethical standards.
