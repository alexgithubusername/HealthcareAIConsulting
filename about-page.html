Alex Dummett, MD, FACP, CPPS

Dr. Alex Dummett, MD, FACP, CPPS, is a nationally recognized physician-informaticist whose career spans clinical practice, medical education, patient safety, and the pioneering integration of artificial intelligence into healthcare. With board certifications in Clinical Informatics, Internal Medicine, and now the AVIM Clinical Informatics Board Certification, he has over a decade of hands-on experience developing, deploying, and optimizing AI-powered clinical decision support tools. As the Founder of Healthcare AI Consulting, LLC, he leads transformative projects that embed evidence-based generative AI into simulation-based training and clinical decision support systems, empowering clinicians to navigate complex care environments with enhanced accuracy and efficiency.

His expertise also extends to telemedicine, innovative early warning systems, and comprehensive quality improvement initiatives – efforts that have not only reduced in-hospital deterioration but have also garnered national recognition (including the prestigious Eisenberg Award). A celebrated educator and mentor, Dr. Dummett has served in roles including Chief Resident, Program Director, and Clerkship Director, guiding the next generation of clinicians in clinical reasoning and technological innovation. Committed to fostering a robust learning health system, he continues to advance the national conversation on the integration of AI in healthcare while delivering direct patient care.
